FrameFit Privacy Policy

Updated June 14, 2026

FrameFit respects your privacy. This page explains how the app handles photos, automatic reframing, social-size preparation, and exported results.

In short: FrameFit does not require an account and does not upload selected photos or exported images to developer servers. Photo analysis, reframing, and export are performed on device by default.

1. Data We Process

Photos you choose, reframed images created in the app, target ratios or platform preferences, temporary processing data needed for export, and image information needed for local composition analysis.

2. On-Device Processing

FrameFit uses local algorithms to analyze the subject, surrounding space, and composition position. By default, this processing happens on your device. FrameFit does not operate developer servers for receiving your photos or exported images.

3. Photos and File Permissions

FrameFit accesses related content only when you choose photos, save images, or export files. You can manage photo and file permissions in iOS Settings.

4. Accounts, Purchases, and Subscriptions

FrameFit currently does not require an account. App Store Connect currently shows no in-app purchases or subscription groups for FrameFit. If paid features are added in the future, we will explain the related handling in this policy or in the app.

5. Third-Party Sharing

FrameFit does not sell your personal information and does not share selected photos with third-party ad networks, except where Apple services are needed for the App Store, system permissions, crash diagnostics, or system operation.

6. Changes and Contact

If this policy changes, we will update the Last Updated date on this page. For privacy or product support, contact LI WEI through https://weili.app/.
