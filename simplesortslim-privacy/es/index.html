Política de privacidad de SimpleSortSlim

Actualizado el 11 de junio de 2026

SimpleSortSlim respeta tu privacidad. Esta página explica cómo gestiona la fototeca, la limpieza de álbumes y el flujo de eliminación.

En resumen: SimpleSortSlim does not require an account and does not operate developer servers for receiving your photos or videos. Filtering, previewing, reviewing, and deletion confirmation happen on your Mac within Apple Photos permissions.

1. Data We Process

Apple Photos library content you authorize, album and folder information, filter settings, review state, built-in trash records, and temporary data needed for previewing, organizing, and deletion.

2. Local Processing

Photo filtering, previewing, keyboard review, album cleanup, and built-in trash handling are performed locally by default. SimpleSortSlim does not upload your photos or videos to developer servers.

3. Photos Permission

SimpleSortSlim needs access to Apple Photos to read media, show previews, organize albums, and perform delete or trash actions only after your explicit confirmation. You can manage Photos permission in macOS System Settings.

4. Deletion and Recovery

The app provides a built-in trash workflow and works with the system trash to reduce accidental deletion risk. Please confirm items before permanently emptying the system trash.

5. Third-Party Sharing

SimpleSortSlim does not sell personal information and does not share your photos or videos with advertising networks, except where Apple services are required for the App Store, system permissions, diagnostics, or Photos framework operation.

6. Contact

For privacy or product support, contact LI WEI through https://weili.app/.
