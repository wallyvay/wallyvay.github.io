FrameCat Privacy Policy and Terms

Updated June 10, 2026

FrameCat respects your privacy. This page explains how the app handles videos, captured frames, stitched long images, and paid features.

In short: FrameCat does not require an account and does not upload selected videos or exported images to developer servers. Frame capture and stitching are performed on device.

1. Data We Process

Videos you choose, frames captured from those videos, stitched long images you create, and temporary processing data needed to export your results.

2. On-Device Processing

Video reading, precise frame navigation, image capture, and long-image stitching are performed on your device by default. FrameCat does not operate developer servers for receiving your video files or exports.

3. Photos and File Permissions

FrameCat accesses related content only when you choose videos, save images, or export files. You can manage photo and file permissions in iOS Settings.

4. Paid Features and Terms

App Store Connect currently shows one paid item for FrameCat: Remove Watermark Lifetime (product ID: framecat.removewatermark). Purchases, refunds, and payment information are handled by Apple. FrameCat does not receive your card or payment credentials. If auto-renewable subscriptions are offered in the future, they will be processed by Apple and can be managed or canceled in the App Store or Apple ID settings. Terms of Use follow Apple's standard EULA: https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/dev/stdeula/

5. Third-Party Sharing

FrameCat does not sell your personal information and does not share selected videos with third-party ad networks, except where Apple services are needed for App Store purchases, diagnostics, or system operation.

6. Contact

For privacy or paid-feature support, contact LI WEI through https://weili.app/.
