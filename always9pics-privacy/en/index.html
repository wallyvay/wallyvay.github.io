Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Always9Pics respects your privacy. This page explains how the app handles photos, exports, the Pro one-time purchase, and user terms.

In short: Always9Pics does not require an account and does not upload selected photos to developer servers. Photo analysis, grouping, and long-image generation run on device by default.

1. Information We Use

To generate 9 long images, the app reads photos you actively select and basic image information such as size, color features, capture time, and album metadata available from the system.

2. Data Collection and Uploads

Always9Pics does not collect names, email addresses, contacts, precise location, or personally identifiable information. The app does not upload your original photos or exported long images to developer servers.

3. Local Processing and Storage

Photo analysis, grouping, ordering, and export are completed on device. Exported long images may be saved to your device, Photos library, or sharing destination you choose. Destination apps process shared content under their own policies.

4. Pro One-Time Purchase

Always9Pics offers Lifetime Pro Membership (product ID: lifetime.pro.always9pics) as a non-consumable in-app purchase. Apple handles purchase processing, refunds, and payment information. Pro unlocks paid features after purchase and does not auto-renew. This in-app purchase currently does not support Family Sharing.

5. User Terms

In addition to this page, Always9Pics uses Apple's standard End User License Agreement (EULA): https://www.apple.com/legal/internet-services/itunes/dev/stdeula/

6. Children's Privacy

Always9Pics is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
