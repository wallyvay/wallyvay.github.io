LivesLong Privacy Policy

Updated June 19, 2026

LivesLong respects your privacy. This page explains how the app handles the photos, Live Photos, videos, previews, and export results you choose.

In short: LivesLong does not require an account. In the current version, selected photos, Live Photos, and videos are not uploaded to developer servers. Stitching, preview, and export are performed on device.

1. Data We Process

Photos, Live Photos, and videos you choose; temporary processing files created for previews and exports; stitching mode, seam style, order, rotation, and trim choices; and diagnostic text you choose to send in feedback email.

2. On-Device Processing

LivesLong uses device capabilities to read selected media for long-image stitching, playable long Live Photo export, video stitching, preview, and export. The current version does not upload selected media to developer servers.

3. Photos Permission

LivesLong accesses related content only when you choose media, save an exported result, or use system sharing. Exported results are saved to your photo library after you choose to export. You can manage Photos access in iOS Settings.

4. Accounts, Purchases, and Subscriptions

LivesLong currently does not require an account. App Store Connect currently shows no in-app purchases or subscription groups for LivesLong. If paid features are added in the future, we will update the related explanation.

5. Third-Party Sharing

LivesLong does not sell your personal information and does not share selected media with third-party ad networks, except where Apple services are needed for the App Store, system permissions, crash diagnostics, or system operation.

6. Changes and Contact

If this policy changes, we will update the date on this page. For privacy or product support, contact LI WEI through https://weili.app/.
